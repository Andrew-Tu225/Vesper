Turn Slack updates into ready-to-post LinkedIn content

Vesper is an AI content assistant for founders and startup teams. It monitors your Slack workspace, classifies launches, customer wins, hiring news, and founder insights, and drafts LinkedIn posts your team can approve in Slack — without leaving the tools you already use.

What Vesper does

Vesper turns Slack messages into ready-to-publish LinkedIn posts. Every signal is classified across five categories: customer praise, product wins, launch updates, hiring announcements, and founder insights. Drafts are delivered to a dedicated Slack channel for one-click review.

Who Vesper is for

  • Founders building a personal brand on LinkedIn
  • Startup marketing teams collecting company updates
  • GTM teams turning launches and customer wins into social proof
  • Employee advocacy and team content workflows

How Vesper works

  1. Connect Slack channels.
  2. Vesper classifies every message and filters operational noise.
  3. Content-worthy signals are drafted into 2–3 LinkedIn post variants.
  4. Drafts arrive in your #social-queue Slack channel for approval, edits, or rewrite.
  5. Approved posts publish to LinkedIn on schedule, with retry on failure.

Use cases

  • Slack to LinkedIn post generator for founders
  • AI thought leadership drafts grounded in real team signals
  • Employee advocacy content automation for startup marketing
  • Repurposing internal product updates into social content
  • Capturing customer wins and turning them into case-study posts

Why Vesper, not a generic AI writer

Generic AI writers start from a blank prompt. Vesper starts from real internal signals — Slack messages, customer feedback, launch updates — and drafts posts grounded in actual team context. Every post is approval-gated, so nothing publishes without explicit human sign-off.
